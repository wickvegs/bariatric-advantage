Frequently Asked Questions

What are bariatric vitamins?

Bariatric vitamins are specially formulated supplements that are designed for individuals who have undergone weight loss surgery such as gastric bypass or sleeve gastrectomy.

What vitamins and minerals are typically included in bariatric vitamins?

Bariatric vitamins typically contain higher doses of vitamins and minerals that are most commonly deficient in bariatric patients, such as iron, calcium, vitamin B12, and folic acid.

Are bariatric vitamins suitable for all weight loss surgery patients?

No, bariatric vitamins are not suitable for all weight loss surgery patients. The specific vitamins and minerals needed and their recommended doses may vary depending on the type of weight loss surgery and individual health status. It is important to consult a healthcare provider for personalized recommendations.
